Big data could be a blanket term for the non-traditional strategies and technologies needed to assemble, organize, process, and gather insights from large datasets. While the matter of working with data that exceeds the computing power or storage of one computer isn't new, the pervasiveness, scale, and value of this kind of computing has greatly expanded in recent years. In this article, we'll speak about big data on a fundamental level and define common concepts you may stumble upon while researching the topic. we'll also take a high-level have a look at a number of the processes and technologies currently being employed during this space
